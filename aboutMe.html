About Me


Oscar Rolando Navarrete Jr is a 20 year old comic artist and character designer born and raised in the Bronx of New York City. With big dreams and aspirations he hopes to make it big in the industry with his projects:

"Ylva and the Devil's Deal"
and
"The Radiant Comet!"

Many of his artworks include illustrations of characters from these two stories, as well as including many illustrations from franchises he enjoys such as Pokemon!
